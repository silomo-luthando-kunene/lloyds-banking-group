# Conclusion
**Focus:** Churn Prevention & Systems Optimization

The "Customer Retention Enhancement" initiative demonstrated the power of data-driven intervention in retail banking. By resolving a critical 4:1 class imbalance using SMOTE and deploying a Random Forest model with 91% recall, the project provides SmartBank with a highly sensitive tool for revenue protection.
* **Business Impact:** The model identifies the vast majority of at-risk "Young Professionals" and "Small Business Owners" before they exit, allowing the Marketing team to deploy targeted loyalty incentives that maximize Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).
* **Systems Engineering Value:** Optimized the customer lifecycle by identifying Login Frequency as a primary systemic failure point. This insight allows for a structural shift in product strategy to increase digital engagement and reduce "retention waste."
